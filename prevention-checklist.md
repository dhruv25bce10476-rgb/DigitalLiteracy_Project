##**GENERAL ONLINE SAFETY**

1.Use strong, unique passwords for every account (mix of letters, numbers, symbols).
2.Enable 2-Factor Authentication (2FA) on Gmail, Instagram, banking apps, etc.
3.Do not click suspicious links in emails, WhatsApp, or SMS (even if they look official).
4.Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive tasks like banking or logging into important accounts.
5.Download apps only from trusted sources like Google Play Store or official websites.
6.Check website URLs carefully (look for HTTPS and correct spelling before entering data).
7.Limit what you share online (college ID, address, phone number, etc.).

Some tips for ##**UPI & FINANCIAL SAFETY**

1.Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone.
2.Do not approve unknown “Collect Request” payments on apps like GPay, PhonePe, or Paytm.
3.Avoid scanning random QR codes—scammers can use them to receive money from you.
4.Ignore calls asking you to verify your account or KYC—banks never ask for sensitive details on calls.

Tips for ##**SOCIAL MEDIA SAFETY**

1.Keep your profiles private and control who can see your posts.
2.Do not accept friend requests from unknown people.
3.Be cautious of fake job or internship offers—verify before applying or paying anything.
